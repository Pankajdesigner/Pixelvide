Our Journey

We are a group of tech enthusiasts motivated to find smart solutions for big problems that our governments face today. Since our inception, we’ve had a difficult time explaining people what we do/what sector do we exactly fall under. Are we a software firm? An app development firm? Are we a E-Governance service provider? Or are we a smart automation company that builds IOT devices?

Our aim is to work on problems that affect at least a million lives. Be it: by building the country’s finest Financial Management and Information System, or our work in the traffic Management and analytics, or building products in land survey to help governments in identity authentication; we are motivated every day when we come to work.

Because we feel that everyday we put in hours together, all the innovation that happens in our workspace, every line of code that we write - are transforming lives even as we read these lines.

As the great philosopher Federiech Neitzche said “the meaning of life is to live authentically and powerfully, creating one's own goals and values” and our meaning of living authentically and powerfully is by solving problems that add value to lives out there; governed by the the biggest organization – the Government!

Culture

Pixelvide is home to some of the most brilliant minds in the nation having graduated from top-tier universities for technology and management. Our pride emanates from people coming together from all over the country, to make a real world impact. The youthful and vibrant atmosphere at Pixelvide is infectious and is the perfect base for the development of projects larger-than-life.
