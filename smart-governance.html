Concept of Smart Governance

We believe that there is enormous potential for development in the government. But this gigantic organization has been resistant to changes in its tried and tested working methodology. It has posed us with herculean challenges in inculcating new approaches.

Over the years we have worked with and helped many governments embrace innovation in ways that were never imagined, and it was only possible because of the smart and innovative technologies, methods that we could bring in. Now and then we would ponder upon the question about what sector or what this sector of work would be called so we coined the term “Smart Governance” to help us identify our work better in the governance space.

Transparency

Introduce in methods/process that makes existing processes more transparent and accountable

Innovation

Think for cost-effective and efficient out of the box solutions.

Efficiency

Build applications that increase the efficiency of all the processes and stakeholders

Social Impact

Build process, applications, methods in a way that will result in tremendous social impact
